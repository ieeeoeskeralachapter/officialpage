Who We Are

The IEEE Oceanic Engineering Society (OES) promotes the objectives of the society around the world through networking, conferences, and publications with a focus on the future.

The Kerala Chapter is dedicated to fostering technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity in the field of oceanic engineering. We bridge the gap between academia and industry.


Our History

The IEEE OES has a rich history spanning over five decades, evolving from the Oceanography Coordinating Committee in 1968 to a global professional organization. The Kerala Chapter continues this legacy by actively engaging in technical activities and student empowerment since its inception.
